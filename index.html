View your remaining balance, recent trips, and other information from contactless public transit cards using your NFC Android phone!

Supported Cards

  • ORCA - Seattle, WA, USA
  • Clipper - San Francisco, CA, USA
  • EZ-Link - Singapore1
  • NETS FlashPay - Singapore
  • Suica, ICOCA, PASMO - Japan
  • OV-chipkaart - Netherlands2
  • Bilhete Único - São Paulo, Brazil2

1 Not compatible with all devices.

2 Requires encryption keys.
